How It Works

Play, compete, and earn SOL every 10 minutes

üéÆ The Game

MADURO.GG is an Agar.io-style multiplayer game where you control a cell that grows by eating food and other players. The twist? You always see yourself as Trump, while everyone else appears as Maduro (and vice versa for them). Everyone is the hero of their own story.

Continuous Play

No rounds, no waiting. Jump in anytime and start playing immediately. The game never stops.

50 Player Arena

Battle against up to 50 players at once. If the arena is full, you'll spectate until a spot opens.

10-Minute Rewards

Every 10 minutes, SOL is distributed to top players on the leaderboard. Automatic and verifiable.

Real Stakes

Connect your Phantom wallet to compete for SOL. No wallet = playing for fun.

üí∞ Reward System

Every 10 Minutes
SOL is distributed to top players

1. Connect Wallet

Link your Phantom wallet before joining the game to be eligible for rewards.

2. Climb the Leaderboard

Eat food and other players to gain mass. Higher mass = higher leaderboard position.

3. Stay in Top Positions

When the 10-minute timer hits zero, a snapshot is taken of the leaderboard.

4. Receive SOL

Top players automatically receive SOL directly to their wallet. No claiming needed.

5. Keep Playing

Timer resets and the game continues. Next distribution in 10 minutes.

Distribution Tiers: Rewards are split among top players based on how many are in the arena. With 2-10 players, top 3 win. With 26-50 players, top 10 win.

Eating: You must be 25% larger than another player to consume them. Smaller cells can hide inside viruses (green spiky circles).

Splitting: Doubles your cells and launches half your mass forward. Great for catching prey or escaping.

Viruses: Feed a virus 7 times (eject mass at it) to spawn a new virus. Large players (150+ mass) explode when touching viruses.

Anti-Snowball: Larger cells decay faster to prevent one player from dominating forever. Killing top players gives bonus mass.
